By Everyone, For Everyone

Welcome to Big Sister based in Boston

We are a safety platform aiming to create a safe space for those who identify as women or non-binary. Providing the ability to report incidents of harassment anonymously and a heat map displaying these incidents, we ensure that you never walk alone

Interactive Heat Map

Our Heat Map displays reports made in your specified area. When you click on a pin, you will be able to see the details regarding the incident of which occurred. You will be able to see the location, the type of incident, and a description of what happened.

Select Your Location

Click the Exlamation Mark in the top right corner of the map to make a report

2022 Harassment Statistics

Harassment on college campuses has been on the rise, and Big Sister is dedicated to decreasing these numbers

78.2% of harassment reports were made by women in 2022

84% of student survivors report being assaulted within the first 4 semesters on campus

Only 12% of student survivors report to the police

23% TGQN (transgender, queer, noncomforming) college students have been sexually assaulted

Provided by rainn.org, knowyourix.org
