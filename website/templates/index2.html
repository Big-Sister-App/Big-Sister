Welcome to based in Boston.

Hello! Welcome to Big Sister, a website designed to harness the inherent sense of community between women and the LGBTQ+ community to keep eachother safe. On this website you can: make a report of harrasment which will display on a map alerting other user in the area; View the harrasement heatmap; View school specific resources; Or join the network to receive alerts. Welcome to the future of saftey technology.

Get to know the Big Sister Team

My roommates friend. Two really smart black girls in my CS lecture (the only other black girls in my CS lecture). And this girl who cracked our first lab assignment with ease, and then didnt write any signatures. That is what we were to eachother at the start. Bonded only by the fact that we were part of the microscopic cohort of black female hackers at Northeastern. But then it was more. Then it was an idea in a study room to better the lives of women. Then it was two unprecedented hackathon wins. It was an article and a venture and a passion. This is PARKA, and we are the founders of Big Sister. Get to know us.
